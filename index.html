How To Play !

In this game there is one ball and two paddles at two ends. Both are user-controlled. Both users have to move the paddle in order not to let the ball pass through, if the ball passes through the left or right side then a point is awarded to the opposing side.

Whichever user is able to obtain the maximum available points of 10 then they will be declared the winner

Player 1 may use the following keys to move: w = up, s = down

Player 2 may use the following keys to move: up, down

Good Luck
